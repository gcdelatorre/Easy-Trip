# 🌍 Easy Trip - AI Travel Planner

**Easy Trip** is a modern travel planning application that turns your "where to go" into a "day-by-day" reality. Just tell us your destination, trip length, group size, and interests, and our AI will build a personalized itinerary for you in seconds.

---

## 🎯 Project Vision (MVP)

### The Problem
Travelers (casual, frequent, solo, families) struggle to plan trips efficiently. Researching destinations, matching interests, and organizing daily activities is time-consuming and often overwhelming.

### Our Solution
**Easy Trip** generates a complete, day-by-day travel plan instantly. By automating the research and organization phases, we save users time and reduce the hassle of trip preparation.

### User Goals
- ✅ Generate a personalized travel plan quickly.
- ✅ View itineraries in a clear, day-by-day format.
- ✅ Save and revisit past or current plans.
- ✅ See destinations and activities on an interactive map.
- ✅ Download plans as a PDF for offline access.
- ✅ Automatic Deep Links for flights, hotels, activities/tours.

- **AI**: Google Generative AI (Gemini API).
